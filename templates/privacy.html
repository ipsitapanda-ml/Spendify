Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

To provide Spendify's expense tracking features, we collect the information you give us directly, such as your name, email address, and login credentials, along with the expense records you log, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to operate and improve Spendify — for example, to display your spending summaries, generate category breakdowns, and keep your account secure. We do not use your expense data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely and retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendify may rely on trusted third-party providers for infrastructure such as hosting and email delivery. These providers only receive the minimum data needed to perform their function and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on your account page.
